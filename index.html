    About

    Started in 1994, Continental Burgers has been proudly serving the best American cuisine for over 23 years in Diamond Bar. We are passionate about serving great quality home-cooked meals while treating you like family. Come on by to enjoy some of our delicious platters and famous sandwiches as you make yourself right at home.
